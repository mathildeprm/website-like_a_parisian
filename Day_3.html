"Electro" exhibition

April 9 - August 11 2019

How to come :
221, avenue Jean-Jaurès
75019 Paris

Do not hesitate to go discover this exhibit; you'll have the opportunity to visit the Paris Philarmonie while learning a bit more about electro music in France. The exhibition is presented through the exploration of its imagination, innovations since 2010, mythologies and its correlations with the visual arts. It is firmly anchored in the present, and is featuring a soudtrack mix by Laurent Garnier, very famouns in Paris and in the world.




Sunday brunch in Montmartre

Previous Next

10 am to 3 pm - Saturdays and Sundays

How to come :
221, avenue Jean-Jaurès
75019 Paris

You cannot leave Paris without having enjoyed a typical Sunday Brunch! In La Bossue, in the heart of Montmartre, you can find this sweet little place, in which you can taste all types of delightful meals from 10 a.m. to 3 p.m.
To get a table in this famous and courted place, you'll have to come sign up for a waiting list. They'll call you when a table is free. During this time, you can enjoy a small walk in the little paved streets around Pigalle and Montmartre.

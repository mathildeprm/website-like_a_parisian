Sunday brunch in Montmartre

Previous Next

10 am to 3 pm - Saturdays and Sundays

How to come : 221, avenue Jean-Jaurès
75019 Paris

You cannot leave Paris without having enjoyed a typical Sunday Brunch! In La Bossue, in the heart of Montmartre, you can find this sweet little place, in which you can taste all types of delightful meals from 10 a.m. to 3
To get a table in this famous and courted place, you'll have to come sign up for a waiting list. They'll call you when a table is free. During this time, you can enjoy a small walk in the little paved streets around Pigalle and Montmartre.
